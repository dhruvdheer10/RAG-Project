Queue Manager Responsibilities

The Queue Manager takes a holistic view of their group’s queues and may conduct some or all of these actions:

Monitors queues regularly, scaling with demand
Reviews queues and assign to appropriate staff
Ensures that every ticket has a resource in the Assigned To field
May re-prioritizes tickets
May acknowledge customer with an initial comment
May use the work notes to provide a warm hand-off to the assigned to user
Reviews the team’s queue at least daily and provide status updates as needed
Reviews aging ticket backlog on a routine basis
Ensures that tickets are processed in a timely manner
Identifies bottlenecks
Analyzes each queue and looks for opportunities to improve turnaround time
Identifies problems that need addressing (routing, aging tickets, lack of work around, etc) and report to ITSM team if needed
